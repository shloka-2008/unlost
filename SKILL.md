# Research Paper Enhancement Skill

Whenever editing or generating content for my research paper, follow these rules automatically.

## 1. Name Correction

Replace every occurrence of:

- Choksi → Chokshi

Apply this correction throughout:
- Author names
- Citations
- References
- Figure captions
- Tables
- Acknowledgements
- Any generated content

Do not change unrelated words.

---

## 2. Academic Writing

Rewrite every section in professional IEEE research paper style.

Requirements:

- Formal academic language
- Third-person writing
- Human-like writing
- No AI-style repetition
- No promotional language
- Good paragraph flow
- Technical accuracy

Improve grammar while preserving meaning.

---

## 3. References

Whenever references are required:

- Include real and relevant references from reputable sources (e.g., IEEE, ACM, Springer, Elsevier, Google Scholar, official documentation).
- Never invent authors, paper titles, journals, DOIs, or publication years.
- If a claim needs a citation and an appropriate source is not known, mark it as "Reference Needed" instead of fabricating one.
- Prefer references on:
  - Lost and Found Systems
  - Inventory Management Systems
  - Smart Campus Applications
  - React.js
  - Node.js
  - MongoDB
  - Google OAuth
  - JWT Authentication
  - Cloud Computing
  - Human–Computer Interaction
  - Information Systems

Generate references in IEEE citation style.

---
